Our Story

At Sasker Auto Glass & Repair, we believe in honest work, fair pricing, and treating our customers like family. What started as a small-town shop has grown into a trusted name in Jackson, Minnesota, known for top-quality glass replacement and auto repair service.

Our Mission

Our goal is simple: to keep you safe and get you back on the road quickly. We take pride in our craft and go the extra mile to ensure every repair meets factory standards and customer satisfaction.

Why Choose Us?

  • Certified technicians using modern equipment
  • Quick turnaround and reliable service
  • Fair, transparent pricing
  • Customer-first approach
